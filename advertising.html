Frequently Asked Questions
What is the difference between Advertising and SEO

SEO is an abbreviation for Search Engine Optimization. It's all about getting the highest rank on certain keywords. SEO ranking take very long time and lots of hard work to get to the first page of a search engine.

Google/Youtube or Facebook/Instagram Ads Work very differently compared to SEO. One is paid advertising and one is passive organic clicks. Paid Advertising always shows above organic listings.

How soon do I start to see results from campaigns?

The short answer is, right away. On occasions it might take a few days to see results.

Should I advertise on Social Media or on a Search Engine?

Each platform has their benefits and drawback. Search Engines are very good for anyone looking to get traffic the same day but not so good for creating a brand Image.
Most of the time advertising on Social Media does not receive results as quickly as Search Engine Ads

Is Advertising worth it?

There are only a few ways to get traffic to your websites. The top most effective way is advertising through search engines or social media platforms
